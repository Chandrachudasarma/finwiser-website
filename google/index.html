What Finwiser is

Finwiser is a digital-native personal finance platform built and operated by Finwiser Technologies LLP, an India-registered limited liability partnership. It is a software product — delivered as an Android app and web experience — that ingests a user's consented financial data, normalises it into a single financial ledger, and generates clear, explainable money decisions.

The advisory layer operates under a SEBI-registered Investment Adviser practice, so the recommendations the product surfaces are fee-only and compliant with Indian investment-advisory regulation.

How the product works

  • Connect once — users link their bank, investment, and other accounts through India's regulated Account Aggregator (AA) consent framework. No screen-scraping, no shared passwords.
  • Unified ledger — consented data is normalised into one financial ledger, with transformer-based categorisation of every transaction.
  • Explainable decisions — the platform runs goal-feasibility modelling and generates natural-language rationale, so each recommendation comes with a plain-English "why".
  • Fee-only advice — delivered under the SEBI Registered Investment Adviser practice, with full regulatory disclosure.
