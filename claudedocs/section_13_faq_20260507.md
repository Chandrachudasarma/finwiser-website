# Section 13 — FAQ

Native HTML accordion. Twelve real user objections answered factually. Zero JS dependency for core open/close (semantic `<details>`); a tiny CSS-only smooth-height enhancement for capable browsers.

---

## 1. Spec

- **Background**: `--white` (#ffffff). Clean, factual, legal-feeling.
- **Heading**: "Questions you're probably asking."
  - Alt 1: "Twelve things people actually ask."
  - Alt 2: "Before you install — read this."
- **Sub-line**: "Asked by real beta users. And, honestly, the founder's family."
- **Layout**: max-width 800px centered. Padding 100px vertical desktop / 64px mobile.
- **Item**: each `<details>` has 1px `--off-white` bottom border. `<summary>` 20px padding, 17px/500. Body 15px / line-height 1.6 / `--text-on-light-muted`.
- **Chevron**: 16x16 SVG right of summary text. Rotates 180deg when open.
- **Smooth open**: CSS `interpolate-size: allow-keywords` + `transition: height` from 0 to `auto`. Browsers without it get the native instant toggle (acceptable fallback — still semantic, still accessible).

## 2. The 12 Q&A pairs (final copy)

1. **What does fee-only mean?** Finwiser earns from your subscription. Not from selling you mutual funds, insurance, or any other product. There's no incentive to push something that's wrong for you. That's the whole point.
2. **How does Finwiser see my accounts without my password?** Through Account Aggregator — an RBI-regulated rail where you grant consent through your AA app, and Finwiser receives read-only data. Finwiser never sees, asks for, or stores your bank password.
3. **Can Finwiser move my money?** No. Read-only access only. Finwiser can see balances and transactions, and give advice. It cannot place trades, transfer funds, or change anything in your accounts.
4. **What if I want to cancel?** Cancel any time inside the app. Withdraw your AA consent. Finwiser loses access immediately. Active data is deleted within 72 hours. No friction, no clawback.
5. **How is this different from my CA / agent?** A CA reviews your numbers once a year and helps with tax. An agent earns commission on what you buy. Finwiser is daily, automatic, fee-only, and won't sell you anything. Your CA still does taxes; Finwiser does the rest.
6. **How is this different from Groww or ET Money?** Those apps help you buy investments — they earn from selling. Finwiser doesn't sell anything. It looks at all your money (not just mutual funds), and tells you what to do — including what to *not* do.
7. **Does it work with my joint account?** Yes. Account Aggregator supports joint accounts. Finwiser can see them once both account holders consent.
8. **Hindi support?** Coming. The app is English-first today. Hindi launches with the public release.
9. **Refund policy?** Free during private beta — no charges yet. Once paid plans go live: cancel before the next billing cycle, no charge for what you haven't used.
10. **What's the SEBI registration number?** INA000021331. Registered as Chandrachuda Sarma Yemmanuru (Proprietor: Finwiser). Verify on SEBI's intermediary search at [sebi.gov.in](https://www.sebi.gov.in/).
11. **If I have a complaint, who do I escalate to?** First: helpdesk@finwiser.org (acknowledged within one working day). Second: SEBI SCORES at [scores.sebi.gov.in](https://scores.sebi.gov.in/). Third: SmartODR at [smartodr.in](https://smartodr.in/). The full grievance pathway is in the footer.
12. **Where's Finwiser based?** Bengaluru, India. Operating from H.No. 181, 14th Cross, Bluejay Atmosphere Phase II, Andhrahalli Main Road, Nagasandra Post, Bengaluru 560073.

## 5. Animation summary

- **Approach chosen**: CSS-only via `interpolate-size: allow-keywords` + `::details-content` height transition. No JS height-measuring helper needed — keeps the section truly progressive. Fallback in older browsers = native instant toggle (still fully usable).
- **Chevron**: 200ms `cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)` rotate to 180deg when `[open]`.
- **Heading**: 8px translateY + opacity fade, 600ms `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)`, triggered by shared site IntersectionObserver toggling `.in-view`.
- **Reduced motion**: all transitions zeroed; heading visible at rest; open/close instant.

---

## 6. Accessibility

- `<section aria-labelledby="faq-heading">` landmark.
- Native `<details>`/`<summary>` — keyboard (Space/Enter) and screen readers handle expand/collapse semantics out of the box; no ARIA polyfill needed.
- Chevron SVG: `aria-hidden="true"` (decorative).
- External links (`sebi.gov.in`, `scores.sebi.gov.in`, `smartodr.in`) open in new tab with `rel="noopener noreferrer"`.
- Focus state: 2px `--teal` outline, 4px offset on `summary` — passes 3:1 against white.
- Contrast: `--text-on-light` (#002d56) on white = 14.7:1. `--text-on-light-muted` (#5a6b7d) on white = 5.8:1. Both pass AA.
- No motion under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`. All content visible regardless.
- Multiple items can be open simultaneously (no forced single-open) — user controls reading flow.
- Reflows cleanly at 320px; tap target on summary is full row (≥48px tall).

Single chevron-down SVG, 16x16, `currentColor` so it inherits `--text-on-light-muted`. Stroke 1.5px, round caps/joins. Rotates 180deg on `[open]` state via CSS — no second icon, no swap. Inline (no sprite, no font).
