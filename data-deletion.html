Data Deletion Request

How to request deletion of your data

How Finwiser Uses WhatsApp

Finwiser uses WhatsApp Business as a communication channel to send you notifications, onboarding messages, and financial insights. WhatsApp is used solely for message delivery — Finwiser does not collect additional personal data from your WhatsApp profile beyond what is necessary for communication (your phone number).

Requesting Deletion of Your Data

If you wish to request deletion of any data associated with your use of Finwiser through WhatsApp or the Finwiser Platform, you may do so by:

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to helpdesk@finwiser.org with the following details:

  • Your registered phone number or email address;
  • A clear statement that you wish to delete your data; and
  • Whether you wish to delete only WhatsApp-related communication data, or your entire Finwiser account and associated data.

Option 2: In-App Request

If you have a Finwiser account, you may request account deletion through the app settings.

Processing Time: Finwiser will acknowledge your request within 3 working days and process the deletion within 30 days, in accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable Indian law.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your phone number and contact details from our WhatsApp communication records;
  • Message delivery metadata associated with your account;
  • If you request full account deletion: all personal data, financial inputs, and derived data as described in our Privacy Policy, Section 6.

Data That May Be Retained

Certain records may be retained after deletion where required under applicable Indian law, including:

  • Records required under SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013 (minimum 5-year retention for advisory records);
  • Records required for fraud prevention, security, or audit purposes; and
  • Records necessary for resolution of pending disputes or grievances.

For full details on data retention and erasure, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
