Why Users Come Back

Financial apps die when users stop opening them. We built 12 retention hooks — all live — informed by behavioral economics research.

Morning Money Pulse

8:30 AM daily — personalized insight from 8 rotating templates. "Your food spending is up 30% this week." Keeps you aware without opening the app.

Daily Question

1 PM — the highest-impact unresolved transaction. "Was this ₹12K to Ajay a loan or a gift?" Your answer improves your entire financial picture.

Advisor Nudges

Proactive, not reactive. Large unusual expense? Spending spike? Fund drifted? We tell you before you notice — with seasonal awareness (Diwali spending isn't an anomaly).

Goal Milestones

"Your emergency fund crossed 50%! Keep going." Positive reinforcement at every milestone. 2:1 ratio — two celebrations for every nudge.

Budget Alerts

Warning at 75%, critical at 90%, overshoot at 100%. Real-time — not a surprise at month-end. CC due date reminders before you get charged interest.

Weekly Portfolio Summary

Goal-anchored progress — "Your retirement is 3% closer." Not raw ₹ swings that cause panic. Designed by behavioral economists to build confidence, not anxiety.

12 retention hooks. All live. Built to keep users coming back without overwhelming them.
