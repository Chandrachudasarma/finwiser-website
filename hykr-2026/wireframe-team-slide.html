Design Rationale

Visual Hierarchy

Founder hero block takes ~65% of vertical space. Co-founder and scale plan are supporting — present but subordinate. The eye reads: name → one-liner → journey → stats → closer.

Timeline Arc

Four steps, left-to-right. Neutral → neutral → red (hardest step) → green (shipped). The gradient connecting line implies intentional progression, not an accident. Each step compounds credibility.

Stats Row = The Punch

Five numbers across five different dimensions: business scale, people scale, teaching scale, building scale, regulatory scale. No other pre-seed founder has all five. This row IS the slide.

Bottom Bar = Closing Argument

Not a summary. A provocation. "A closer who built the product" forces VCs to update their mental model. "Imagine what happens with ₹2 Crore" is the call to action — delivered as a dare.

Color System

Pink (#ff6b81) = past credentials, proven. Green (#00b894) = done, shipped, forward-looking. The color shift from pink stats to green stats mirrors the journey from operator to builder.

Post-Funding Card

Green border answers the VC's unasked question: "what does this founder do next?" before they ask it. Shows self-awareness — founder knows when to code and when to sell.
