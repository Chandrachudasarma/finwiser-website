## [0:00 – 0:06] Intro — Finwy says Hi
> Hey, hi! I'm Finwy — your AI-powered financial advisor.

## [0:06 – 0:12] Opening Problem
> Most Indians cannot see their full financial life in one place.
> Their money is scattered across accounts, investments, loans, cards, emails, and statements.

## [0:12 – 0:18] Product Promise
> Finwiser brings this data together and turns it into regulated, goal-based financial advice.

## [0:18 – 0:30] Login + Consent (compact)
> The user signs in with OTP — no passwords, no manual setup.
> Through the Account Aggregator framework, she connects her financial accounts using consent-based data sharing.
> No screen scraping. No password sharing.

## [0:30 – 0:49] Financial Picture *(extended)*
> In seconds, Finwiser builds her real financial picture — net worth, investments, liabilities, cashflow, and planning surplus.
> No manual estimates.

## [0:49 – 1:01] Risk Profile
> Before advice, Finwiser captures her risk profile.
> It combines willingness to take risk with actual financial capacity, so recommendations are suitable for her situation.

## [1:01 – 1:15] Portfolio Advice
> Finwiser evaluates every mutual fund for performance, peer rank, risk, suitability, and fit with her profile.
> The output is simple: keep, review, or replace.

## [1:15 – 1:32] Cashflow Advisor
> For cashflow, Finwiser does not guess.
> When a transaction is unclear, the advisor asks: was this salary, reimbursement, transfer, or something else?
> Her answers improve categorisation and reveal her real monthly surplus.

## [1:32 – 1:41] Surplus Lock
> She confirms the surplus using the slider.
> Once locked, this becomes the planning number.
> The system does not silently override the user.

## [1:41 – 2:00] Goals + Recommendations
> Emergency Fund and Retirement are mandatory goals.
> The user can also add custom goals with target amount, priority, and deadline.
> Finwiser calculates the SIP required and screens funds through a suitability matrix built around SEBI mutual-fund categories.
> Short-term goals get liquid, low-risk options. Long-term goals get growth-oriented options.

## [2:00 – 2:05] Outro
> Every recommendation is goal-specific, risk-aware, and based on real financial data.
> Finwiser is live on Google Play Closed Testing.
