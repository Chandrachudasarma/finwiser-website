The innovation

Real financial advice was never one app — it was a team: a goal planner, a portfolio analyst, a cashflow analyst, and an adviser to tie it together. The wealthy hire that team. Everyone else gets dashboards that show data and sell products.

Finwiser is that entire team, delivered as one AI — the first SEBI-registered advisory experience in India to unify goals, cashflow, portfolio and suitability into a single decision. We call the engine Next Best Rupee™.

Connect

Link bank, mutual funds, FDs, NPS, loans and credit — once, through the RBI Account Aggregator. No passwords, no manual entry.

Detect

Normalise everything into one ledger, run 13 financial-health ratios, and find the family's real investable surplus.

Direct

Allocate that surplus across the family's prioritised goals and recommend the exact suitable next action — explained in plain words.

No other app in India can say this — because nobody else holds the family's full picture and a fiduciary licence to act on it.
