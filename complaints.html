How to Lodge a Complaint

If you have a grievance regarding any advisory service provided by Finwiser, you may lodge it through any of the following channels. Full details are in the Investor Charter — Grievance Redressal section.

Step 1: Contact Finwiser

  • Email: chandrachudasarma@gmail.com or helpdesk@finwiser.org
  • Mobile: +91 8801294657
  • Grievance Redressal Officer: Chandrachuda Sarma Yemmanuru (Proprietor)
  • Acknowledgement: Within 1 working day. Resolution target: 7 days. Outer limit: 21 days.

Step 2: Escalate to IAASB (BASL)

If unresolved, contact BSE Administration & Supervision Limited (IAASB) at P. J. Towers, Dalal Street, Mumbai – 400 001. Website: www.bseasl.com.

Step 3: SEBI SCORES

Lodge a complaint on the SEBI Complaints Redress System: scores.sebi.gov.in. SCORES is SEBI's centralised online platform for complaints against any registered intermediary.

Step 4: SMART ODR

For online mediation, conciliation, or arbitration: smartodr.in.

Exercising the right to escalate will not result in any adverse action by Finwiser. All complaints are treated equally, regardless of the channel.

Definitions & Methodology

  • Complaint: Any written or verbal expression of dissatisfaction by a client (or prospective client) regarding any aspect of Finwiser's advisory services.
  • Received: Complaints logged in the Grievance Register during the calendar month, regardless of channel.
  • Resolved: Complaints for which a written Final Response has been sent to the client and the complaint marked closed in the register.
  • Pending: Complaints in the register that have been received but not yet marked resolved.
  • Average Resolution Time: Sum of days taken to resolve each complaint resolved in the period, divided by the number of complaints resolved. “NA” indicates no complaints were resolved in the period.
  • Carried Forward / Opening: Complaints pending at the start of the financial year that remained unresolved from the previous year.

The methodology aligns with SEBI's complaint-disclosure requirements for Investment Advisers. Grievance-register details are retained for a minimum of five years, as required under Regulation 19 of the SEBI (IA) Regulations, 2013.
